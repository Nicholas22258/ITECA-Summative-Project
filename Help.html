Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell?

1. Sign in here. If you don't have an account, create one here
2. Click the plus button under your "Your Items:" heading.
3. Fill in the relevant information about the item you wish to sell. Please note that you can only upload one picture of the item.
4. Click "Add Item".

How do I buy?

NickC2C does not have any built-in payment services. In order to buy an item, you must contact the owner of that item via his/her provided email address. Payments are negotiated between you and the seller, and occur independent of NickC2C.

Is this a real website?

NickC2C is a real website, and can be used for customer-to-customer e-commerce. However, the items you see here are fake and is being used as dummy data for a project.

Does this website use cookies?

NickC2C uses cookies to function properly.
