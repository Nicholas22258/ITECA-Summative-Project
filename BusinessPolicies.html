Business Policies

Although NickC2C has little control over what users want to sell, we do have various measures to prevent criminal activity on the platform. When users wish to sell an item, the item's details is examined by our administrator to look for potential discrepancies in the item. Any items determined to be unacceptable will be rejected from being added to the platform. Our administrators also constantly learn about the latest scams so that they can keep an eye out for these potential scams on this platform. We do not consent to selling items that are considered to be illegal, dangerous, medicinal (that requires a prescription) or pornographic in nature. Any violation of this rule will result in the account holder being banned and his/her items removed from this site.
